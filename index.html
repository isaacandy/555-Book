555 User Manual

1. The Handshake

To ensure trust, 555 uses a "Double-Approval" system. A debt is only real when both parties agree. Link your book via WhatsApp to start the secure exchange.

2. The Ledger

Track Hard Debt (Money) for transactional things like bills, and Soft Debt (Favors) for relationship capital like airport rides or chores.

3. Face-Saving Mode ü§´

When you quietly help someone to protect their pride, log it as "Face-Saving." These are strictly private and encrypted in your Vault. They never appear on the public wall.

4. Settle Up

Repay your friends by offering a favor from your pre-built catalog. Once they acknowledge the favor, your balance re-adjusts automatically.
